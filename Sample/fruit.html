Types of fruits

Citrus fruits

Berries

Seasonal fruits

Citrus fruits are a group of juicy, tangy-flavored fruits like oranges, lemons, and limes that grow on trees, bushes, or shrubs. They are known for being rich in Vitamin C and antioxidants, which help support the immune system and provide protective benefits
Berries are small, pulpy, and often edible fruits, rich in antioxidants, vitamins, fiber, and minerals that offer numerous health benefits, including improved heart and digestive health. Common examples include strawberries, blueberries, raspberries, and blackberries
Seasonal fruits are those that are naturally ripe during a specific time of year, like mangoes in the summer, oranges in the winter, and strawberries in the spring. Different fruits are abundant in summer (mangoes, watermelon), monsoon (jamun, plums), autumn (pears, grapes), and winter (apples, guavas, oranges). Trees that are slow to grow bear the best fruit
