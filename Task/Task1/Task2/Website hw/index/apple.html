Apple

An apple is the round, edible fruit of an apple tree.
Fruit trees of the orchard or domestic apple, the most widely grown in the genus, are cultivated worldwide.
The tree originated in Central Asia, where its wild ancestor, Malus sieversii, is still found.

Benefits of Apple

  • The soluble fiber and polyphenols in apples can help lower cholesterol and blood pressure, while potassium helps manage blood pressure
  • Apples are low in calories but high in fiber and water, which makes them filling and can help reduce overall calorie intake
  • Apples contain pectin, a type of fiber that acts as a prebiotic, feeding beneficial gut bacteria.
