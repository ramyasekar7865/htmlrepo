Banana

A banana is a sweet, elongated, edible fruit that grows in bunches on large, herbaceous plants of the genus Musa
The most common type is the dessert banana, but other varieties called plantains are starchier and used for cooking.
Bananas are rich in nutrients likeassium, vitamin B6, and vitamin C

Benefits of Banana

  1. Bananas are a good source of dietary fiber, which promotes regular bowel movements.
  2. They contain prebiotic fiber, which feeds beneficial gut bacteria.
  3. Pectin, a type of fiber in bananas, can help soften stool.
