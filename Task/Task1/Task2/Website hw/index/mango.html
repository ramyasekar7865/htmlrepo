Mango

Mangoes are sweet, tropical drupe fruits native to South Asia, known for their orange flesh and single large, flat seed
They are rich in vitamins A and C and are used in a wide variety of dishes, from sweet desserts to tangy pickles and chutneys
There are many varieties, including Alphonso, Kesar, and Dasheri.

Benefits of Mango

  1. Mangoes are rich in Vitamin C, Vitamin A, and other antioxidants that strengthen the immune system by helping to produce white blood cells and protect against infections.
  2. They provide essential vitamins and minerals like Vitamin C, Vitamin A, Vitamin K, folate, copper, and B6.
  3. They contain a variety of antioxidants, including carotenoids, polyphenols, and mangiferin, which help protect against cell damage and may lower the risk of certain cancers
